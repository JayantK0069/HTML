About Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is a flavorful and healthy dish made by cooking marinated chicken over an open flame, grill, or griddle. The marinade often includes ingredients like olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and spices, which enhance the chicken's taste and keep it tender. Grilled chicken is a versatile meal that pairs well with salads, vegetables, or rice, making it a popular choice for both casual meals and special occasions.
